About Me

I've been working in the gaming industry for the past 11 years. During this time I've worked at Electronic Arts, Zynga Games and Glu Mobile as Game Designer. I've worked on different genres of games which include - Match 3 Puzzle, Casino Slots, Shooter, Social Farming Simulator. I'm good at Unity and some other game engine editors such as Creation Kit by Bethesda Games and Dunia Engine by Ubisoft games. I'm a gamer turned game designer to create fun experiences for players. I spend my time creating games, learning new tools and playing various video games. My other interests are Technology, Space, Lord of the Rings and Star Wars.
